About My Work

I'm Sytze, graduated in Geo-Information Science from Wageningen University with a background in education.


It is my mission to make technology understandable and usable for everyone - regardless of technical background. I believe organizations should be able to work independently with their own data, without being dependent on opaque systems or expensive licenses.


In addition to my independent work, I run the company Enoki together with Kamiel Verhelst, where we help organizations with responsible AI implementation.
